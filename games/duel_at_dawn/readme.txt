# Duel At Dawn

A 5x5 grid slot game with 19 fixed paylines featuring VS Duel and Outlaw mechanics.

## Game Features

- **5x5 Grid**: 5 reels × 5 rows
- **19 Fixed Paylines**: Various patterns including horizontal, W-shaped, M-shaped, and diagonal lines
- **VS Symbol (DuelReels™)**: Expands to wild reel, triggers duel with multiplier (2x-200x)
- **Outlaw Symbol**: Expands to wild reel, shoots 1-6 random wilds with multipliers (2x-200x)
- **Wild Wild West Bonus**: 3 FS scatters = 10 free spins
- **Dusk 'Til Dawn Bonus**: 4 FS scatters = 10 free spins with guaranteed VS symbols

## Base Game

Standard lines game with VS and Outlaw symbols that can trigger their respective features.

## Free Game

Two different free spin modes:
- **Wild Wild West**: Standard free spins with normal feature frequency
- **Dusk 'Til Dawn**: Enhanced free spins with guaranteed VS symbols and higher multipliers
